YOUR CASE IS OUR CASE

Connecticut is full of good attorneys. What sets me apart from my competition is the diversity of my practice areas and the depth of my knowledge base. Most uniquely, however, is that once retained, I will not give-up on you. I will strive to win your case or settle it the way you want.

The legal system presents a number of challenges for lay persons but I have been around for a while so I know how to get things done. I will try to handle your case as quickly and painlessly as possible. I know you want to get on with your life or respective business interests.

The facts of your case matter to me. Professionally meeting your legal needs is my number one goal every day. I care about you while handling your divorce, keeping a family member out of jail, listening when you have been wrongly discharged from your employer or helping negotiate a favorable settlement. Simply stated, I care about you.

Diversity Training

In this course, you will learn about affirmative action policy in the United States. It contains audio and visual presentations, as well as supplemental materials to enhance your learning experience. The course contains several comprehensive lectures and it should take a few hours to complete. It begins with a detailed explanation of Affirmative Action policy, then moves to Title VII of the Civil Rights Act of 1964 and concludes with a sample Affirmative Action Plan.
